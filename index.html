How to play
1
You are given a start station and a destination.
2
Build your route selecting stations and lines one stop at a time. You must change lines at least once.
3
Your final stop must be the destination. Then hit Submit my route.
4
Your route is scored against the optimal journey time.

New · Hard mode BETA
Two daily puzzles now: 🟢 Easy and 🔥 Hard. Switch between them using the tabs above the puzzle.
Hard puzzles require 2+ line changes for the optimal route - the obvious single-change route won't be enough.
Hard mode is in beta. If a puzzle feels unfair or its optimal route looks weird, please send feedback - it really helps us tune the difficulty.
What's new
Wait times added (June 2026): the optimal route now factors in the average wait for each line change, on top of walking time. Wait values come from TfL off-peak frequencies (Mon-Fri 10:00-15:00 timetable). This means the "optimal" is closer to what a real Londoner would take, sometimes one more change saves time, sometimes it doesn't.
Smarter hints (June 2026): Hint 2 now reveals the first letter of your first interchange station — a playful nudge toward the trickiest part of the puzzle without giving the whole route away. Hints 1 and 3 still show the lines at your start and end.
Puzzles refresh at midnight. Each change includes walking time + average wait for the next train (based on TfL off-peak frequencies). Same puzzles for everyone worldwide.
