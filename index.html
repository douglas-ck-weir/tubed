How to play
1
You are given a start station and a destination on the London Underground.
2
Build your route by selecting stations and lines one stop at a time. You must change lines at least once — no single-line routes!
3
Your final stop must be the destination station. Then hit Submit my route.
4
Your route is scored against the optimal journey time. The closer you are, the better your medal.
Scoring
🥇Within 2 minutes of optimal — perfect!
🥈Within 5 minutes of optimal
🥉Within 10 minutes of optimal
⭐Within 20 minutes of optimal
🚇More than 20 minutes off — keep practising!
A new puzzle is available every day at midnight. Times are travel time only — no waiting or walking time is included. Everyone worldwide gets the same puzzle each day.
